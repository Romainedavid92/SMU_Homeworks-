Summary: City Latitude vs. City Wind Humidity

There is not a great relationship between latitude and Temperature shown by the graph. It just seems to be acceptable to have pit stains no matter where you are. But, there is an interesting area between -20 and 20 degrees latitude, where it almost appears a triangle was cut out of the graph. This is most likely explained by extreme temperatures along the equator in dry areas.
